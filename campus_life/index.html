Campus life

Ganpat University is a place where ideas, passions, and questions can be fully explored. With an overwhelming number of campus clubs and student organizations, you’ll find something for every interest and passion. There’s so much to do, and countless opportunities to connect, nourish talents, and serve others. A current student put it best: GNU is for students who were over-committed in high school.”

Though located in a rural area; in a North Gujarat region, approximately 70 km from Ahmedabad; University has set up an exemplary example of “Uplifting the Society through Education” by developing world-class social infrastructure with a global vision.

Weather

Ganpat University's main campus is a part of the North Gujarat region. This region has a temperate climate with diverse conditions and four distinct seasons. With mild winter, moderate monsoon and her summer, this region make it comfortable mostly in all seasons.

Diversified Culture & Communities

GNU residence halls offer plenty of personal space to stretch, study, and connect. New students share their first year experience with others from diverse backgrounds—47 states and 37 countries!—and all take part in fun activities to break from studying and get to know other students.

Library Computerization

All libraries are powered by SOUL 2.0 (INFLIBNET) library management software for the development of in house databases and automation for well-managed functioning of library like acquisition, cataloguing, books transaction, serials control etc

Online Public Access Catalog (OPAC)

OPAC is widely used as a search tool. Information about all items in University Central Library collection is available via OPAL. This gives an opportunity to the learners to use OPAL extensively and frequently to check and verify available titles online.

Digital Repository

Ganpat University Institutional Digital repository contains a wide range of material like Ph. D. Thesis, Dissertation Report, Project Report, E-books, Lecture notes, Newsletter, Expert lecture, Video lectures, Paper clipping, Presentation, journal articles, Conference papers, Working papers etc.

Library Collection

The Libraries' collections support the teaching and research mission of the campus and provide the wide platform to connect the current trend of curriculum. The Libraries at each institute is connected with the University Central Library, which has more than 1.20 Lacs top quality printed collection and provide access to approximately 500 national and international level printed periodicals. All libraries also maintain a collection of Ph.D. Thesis, Dissertations, Project Reports, Bound Volumes and CD-ROMs. Libraries provide digital access of affluent e-resources like ProQuest, ii-Gate, McGraw Hill's Access Engineering, Science Direct (Elsevier), ASTM Digital Library, IEL Growth Plan (IEEE), ASME, ASCE etc.

Library Resources

The Ganpat University Central Library contains a wide range of material that reflects the intellectual wealth of the University and its constituent institutions.
